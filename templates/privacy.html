Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Landmark (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related backend services (collectively, the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and an encrypted password. You may also provide a profile picture (optional).
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise GPS coordinates to show nearby business leads and offer location‑based features. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.
  • Business Lead Data: When you invite a business, we collect the business name, phone number, category, city, and its geo‑coordinates. This information is stored to help others discover local businesses.
  • Referral Data: We track which users refer businesses and compile anonymous leaderboards (your name and referral count may be shown publicly if you participate).
  • Payment Information: If you make payments through our Service, payments are processed by Razorpay, a third‑party payment gateway. We do not store your full credit/debit card details on our servers. Razorpay shares a transaction ID and status with us to confirm successful payments.
  • Usage Data: Our backend may automatically log your IP address, browser/device type, and pages visited for security and debugging. This data is deleted periodically.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To enable location‑based features (nearby leads, heatmaps).
  • To display business leads to other users (only business contact info, never your personal account details).
  • To process referral rewards and show leaderboards.
  • To process payments securely via Razorpay.
  • To communicate with you regarding service updates, security alerts, and support.
  • To improve and monitor our Service (debug logs).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third parties to host our Service (e.g., Render for backend hosting) and to process payments (Razorpay). These providers only receive the data necessary to perform their functions and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law or to protect our rights, we may disclose information to law enforcement.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred as a business asset.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Business lead data remains in the system indefinitely to help the community, but you can request removal by contacting us. Logs are automatically deleted after 30 days.

5. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are hashed and salted. All communication between your app and our server is encrypted via HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Future Features (WhatsApp Marketing)

In the future, we may add optional marketing messages through WhatsApp. Before that feature goes live, we will update this Privacy Policy and clearly inform you. You will always have the choice to opt in or out.

8. International Data Transfers

Our Service is currently intended for users in India. All data is stored and processed on servers located in India (or the hosting region selected on Render). If you access the Service from outside India, your information may be transferred to our servers.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also withdraw consent for location services at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website or within the app.
